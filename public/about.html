About Me

So who is Oni Khalid Ayòbámi?

Like every other person, I'm a lot of things. I'm Khalid, an architecture student, a front-end developer, graphic designer, lover of football, basketball, music and languages etc.



⯈Khalid, The graphic designer

My passion for graphic design comes from a life-long fascination with seeing beautifully designed things and wanting to make them. As a kid, I was always fascinated by beautiful event posters I saw on the streets of Lagos, the illustrations in my textbooks and my favourite comic book Supa Strikas, I really wanted to be able to make similarly beautiful designs, so I developed a hobby of making pen doodles and drawings.

The simple act of putting together different forms and colors to create beautiful things brings me so much satisfaction. I believe I can be any kind of designer I wish to be which is why I'm currently a student of Architecture at OAU and why in 2019 I decided to pursue a career in graphic design. I've since finished a graphic design certificate course from Geneza SOD, been working as a freelance graphic designer. Check out some of my Design Works



⯈Khalid, The front-end dev

I remember wanting to create my own website and facebook-like app back in my secondary school days, wanting to build a more beautiful social media app that's going to replace facebook. Enthusiastic, while w orking with every website builder I could find on the internet, I had a lot of fun creating websites on my mum's java phone, enjoying every bit of it. Soon started exploring sites like Wix and Squarespace although I only did it as a hobby.

Fast forward to 2021 when I came across a front-end development course on Coursera, Although I had an idea of what HTML and CSS were, I had only ever used website builders, I was scared at the thought of coding because of the terrible things I've heard about 'programming' but the more I explored, the more my interest in it grew, I started enjoying coding and I haven't looked back since. Check out some of my Dev Works



⯈Khalid, Outside work

Yes, I love the work I do on the web and can be a workaholic working extra hours to get the work done but that's not all there is about my life. When I’m not keeping busy with coding or design, I enjoy learning languages and meeting new people from all over the world, I currently speak English, Yorùbá and Spanish really well and I also speak a little bit of French and Italian. I write sometimes - about design, fiction, frontend development, language learning and life.
